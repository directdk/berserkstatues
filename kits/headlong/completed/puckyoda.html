Puck: Yoda
- by Headlong
- scale 1/2
- limitation < 60
- project finished 3.9.05
- estimated value of kit alone: $25+
- pictures uploaded 3.9.05
- painter: John Allred

Haha, YODA PUCK!!!  I got to hand it to Headlong for sculpting such a hilarious kit.  And of course, I have to hand it to John Allred for painting it so well.  I really like how he painted the inside clothing.  He made the texture look ragged and worn.  John also custom sculpted some wrinkles in Puck's face to help add more character.

One thing that I also extremely appreciate is how John made it so this Puck kit can fit fluidly on the column of the Guts: Black Swordsman base, but also interchangeably on his own custom base (the tree stump).  John even carved in rings!  Man, he's so cool =P.
