To those of you who saw the anime, but have not read the manga, you are probably unfamiliar with this scene, but this actually takes place BEFORE the Eclipse.  For some reason or another, it was omitted from the anime.  I'm not surprised though, because it's a rather gory and disturbing duel, and it might have taken away some of the impact of the Eclipse if it wasn't deleted.  Nevertheless, this is a great scene, especially when Guts stabs and cuts off Wyald's penis (or giant tentacle... something of the sort).  Ahhh, Kentarou Miura, what a genius =).
