This is the 2nd Berserk statue I ever bought, even before Toycom started releasing it.  As a whole, I'm satisfied with the statue: it's a clean, accurate sculpt.  However, if I had one complaint, it would be the eyes.  From what I've noticed, Art of War does a good job with eyes, except for when the eyes are larger and more "anime" style.  I think AOW is best suited for creating models of warriors and monsters, instead of hot babes like Casca. =)  Ah well, what statue can EVER really capture the beautiful Casca, eh?
