Ah! My Goddess: Belldandy: Wind
- by Kaiyodo
- scale 1/8
- serial # 83
- limitation < 300
- estimated value: $500+
- pictures uploaded 1.22.05

For all non-Berserk statues, I would definitely have to say that this is my GRAIL statue.  I've seen this sculpt as a kit before, but never knew it also exists as a pre-painted statue by Kaiyodo.  I couldn't be more pleased with this gorgeous display piece.  Belldandy's wingspan is HUGE, hovering almost two feet!  Try to visualize that my Berserk statues are generally 1/10 scale, and this piece is 1/8!  And with all that extra hair and clothing flying all over the place, it's quite insane.  I must thank my lucky stars that I own this statue.  In terms of rarity and value, it's the only AMG statue pretty much on par with the famous Holy Bell by Volks.
