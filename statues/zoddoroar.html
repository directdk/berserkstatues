Zoddo: Apostle II (Limited Edition - Bloody with Silver Skullknight Bust)
- scale 1/15
- serial # 413
- limitation 300
- estimated value: $500+
- pictures uploaded 9.6.05

When Art of War announced they were releasing a second Zoddo Apostle statue, I wasn't sure what to think.  I didn't want to get my hopes up because I didn't think any Zoddo statue could compare to the first one.  Well... I was surely mistaken, because this statue absolutely ROCKS!

It's the same scale as the first Zoddo Apostle statue, which is a plus.  They look beautiful standing side by side, TOWERING over all the other Berserk statues.  There are no wings on this statue, but the sculpt itself is more than enough to steal your attention anyways.

The details of the muscle and fur are outstanding, but the mouth and teeth are just incredible!  Man oh man, I feel sorry for all those poor souls that fall victim to this MONSTER.  Just look at Zoddo roaring!  Vicious... purely vicious.

The bloodwork is subtle, but just enough.  I'm not a fan of the bloodwork on the first Zoddo statue, because it looks too overdone and thick.  However, I'm very pleased with the way this second statue turned out and I'm definitely glad I decided to buy this version instead of the non-bloody one.

In addition, both exclusive editions come with a Skullknight Bust (white or silver).  Since I got the bloody version of Zoddo, I'm awarded the silver bust and I couldn't be more happier.  Actually... that's a lie.  I would be happier if the bust was a FULL figure because it's damn cool!  I reallllly dig the way they painted it.  A part of me wishes they painted their other Skullknight statues this way!

So as you can see, this is an AWESOME statue, and a MUST-HAVE for any Berserk statue collector.  And as with all the others, the sooner you get one, the better.  Look at the inflated prices of the first Zoddo statue.  You don't want to wait to buy this second one because I'm sure the value will ONLY go up!
