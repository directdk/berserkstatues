Guts: Hawk Soldier: Maken (Limited Edition - Bloody)
- scale 1/10
- serial # 213
- limitation 300
- estimated value: $200+
- pictures uploaded 1.22.05

Man, Guts looks torn up in this sculpt.  Poor Guts, he never gets a break. =(

This statue ideally belongs with the Wyald statue to mimic the Death Match duel right before the Eclipse.  This isn't my favorite Guts statue, but it's nevertheless still cool, especially when paired up with Wyald.  I think, overall, I don't have any complaints about the piece, except for maybe the blood on the neck.  You can't really tell in my pictures, but the blood sort of looks like Guts has the chicken pox or something.  That's probably not the best way of putting it, but I feel that the blood on the neck just doesn't look realistic.  Definitely not Art of War's best work in regards to blood.

Other than that, this is another solid statue from Art of War, and it's one of the select few that have been imported to the US.  So, you can potentially get this one for a pretty good price if you look hard enough!
