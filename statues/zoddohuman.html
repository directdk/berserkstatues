Zoddo: Human
 - scale 1/10
- serial # 494
- limitation < 2000
- estimated value: $150+
- pictures uploaded 2.6.05

Man, what a giant mass of muscle Zoddo is.  Even in his "human" form, he's still a monster.  A monster with veiny arms, red eyes, and sharp teeth.  And that's exactly what Art of War created: a super buff statue with veiny arms, red eyes, and sharp teeth!  Right on!  =)

Other than those little details, this statue is well done and quite accurate overall.  I appreciate the fact that AOW also includes Zoddo's sword sheath as well as a sword to lay on the base to signify (from what I can guess) one of the Hawks' swords at the Reunion on the Hill of Swords.

If I were to have one complaint, is I think the pose is a bit stiff looking.  From the side/back angle, he's sort of leaning forward and that, to me, makes him look a little off balance and awkward.  However, from the front or bottom-to-top angle, Zoddo looks GOOD (and a little scary).
