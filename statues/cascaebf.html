Casca: Episode of Birth Feast
 - scale 1/10
- serial # 609
- limitation < 2000
- estimated value: $175+
- pictures uploaded 2.24.05

Though simple, this is one of my favorite Berserk statues.  I love Casca, and I think Art of War did a great job replicating the confused look she always has since the horrid Eclipse.  There is also quite a lot of detail in the dress and I think the eyes are done better than the eyes on the Casca: Hawk Soldier statue.  Also, one thing that is pretty neat is that the bottom of each foot is actually painted dirty, something I think is cool since in plain sight you can't really see it, but AOW painted it that way anyways for the sake of being thorough.
