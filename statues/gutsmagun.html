It boggles my mind that the limited edition statue took so long to sell out on the Art of War website.  This statue is GREAT!  Actually, I think it might have something to do with the way the blood looks like in pictures.  Let me try to describe it in more detail.

Since it is raining in the corresponding scene of the manga, the blood is supposed to look diluted and watery, running down the sword and body like it is on the statue.  This is, obviously, the look that Art of War was going for which is why the blood looks the way it does in pictures.  However, as it is the case many times, the statue and the blood look much better in person than in the pictures online.  There are even mud splashes on the pants, shoes and underside of the cape!  This piece is VERY well done, and all of you that have putting off getting this one, should DEFINITELY get off your butt and get one now.
