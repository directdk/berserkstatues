Slan and Guts
- scale 1/10
- serial # 155
- limitation 300
- estimated value: $350+
- pictures uploaded 12.15.05

A masterpiece!  Simply one of my favorite Art of War statues, no question!  The amount of detail and intricacies on this piece is beyond the call of duty.  The veins, the roots of Slan's wings, her hair, Guts' scars, his belt, the troll intestines on the ground, the blood... I can go on and on!  And kudos to the sculptor of this statue.  The statue is full of intensity, I love it!  I just love this statue!

There are a few shortcomings of this statue, but they are VERY minor.  The first being the "bendiness" of Slan's right wrist (it looks slightly stretched), and the second being the faces of both characters.  There is nothing wrong with the faces, in my opinion, but they don't look 100% accurate.  But really, none of Art of War's non-demonic faces look entirely perfect anyways, so it's really not a big issue.  Actually, I think Slan's face in this statue looks insanely spooky, which is a GOOD thing.  Sure, it could have been more sexy, but hey, that's why she's butt naked and slimy!  She's pretty hot for a woman inhabiting a body made of troll intestines, if you ask me!

As you can see, I went a little picture happy so there are tons of pictures and angles for your viewing pleasure.  I really tried my best to capture the statue's beauty, but alas, my camera skills can only do so much.  I can't recommend this statue enough.  It really belongs in everyone's Berserk collection.  If you don't own this piece, then you should be ashamed of yourself!  Get it now!!!  Sell your first born if you must!
