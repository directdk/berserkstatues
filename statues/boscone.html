Boscone
 - scale 1/10
- serial # 472
- limitation < 2000
- estimated value: $150+
- pictures uploaded 2.5.05

Originally, I didn't intend to buy this statue.  From the pictures I saw of it, I was a little unimpressed by its static-looking pose.  However, Boscone is a character that I later started to respect (as I re-read the manga for a second time), because in my opinion, he is really the only noble and honorable warrior of Tudor.  However, after receiving him, I've grown to appreciate this statue since it's actually a rather large one, and the armor and cape look quite nice in person.  The ridiculously tall weapon also adds some flare to the piece and Art of War did a decent job replicating the picture from the manga.

Although the normal edition doesn't include the extra head with no helmet (like Exclusive Edition I does), I felt like buying this one because I prefer to display this piece with his helmet on.  However, seeing how this statue was imported to America, I may pick up a limited edition if I find a good price.
