Puck: Kuri: Smiling (Limited Edition)
 - scale 1/1
- no serial
- limitation 300
- estimated value: $60+
- pictures uploaded 2.4.05

Contrary to the action figure version of this sculpt, this Smiling resin statue is extremely hard to find.  Only 300 of these limited edition variants were made.  It's pretty hard to tell the difference between the statue version and the action figure version, but of course, the resin one is heavier and more valuable.  However, unless you are a die-hard collector, the action figure version does the job perfectly fine.

Additionally, there is a mini-action figure version from Figure Set II that is 2-3 times smaller, which actually fits nicer with the other AOW statues since the relative scale is more accurate.  Unfortunately, that one is also pretty hard to find.  And lastly, there is the silver exclusive resin variant that's quite neat and even more scarce.
