Griffith: Millennium Falcon on Horse
- scale 1/15
- serial # 601
- limitation < 2000
- estimated value: $250+
- pictures uploaded 1.13.05

First of all, ANY horse-riding statue always deserves four stars. =)  This one is no exception.  It may be a little on the small side, but like the other 1/15 scaled Berserk statues, the detail is still intact.  I especially like how the horse was done, and how clean and white it reflects light.  When I think Griffith, I think pure pristine white.  This is one of the main reasons why I chose to buy the normal edition over the limited edition.  Again, I like to stay as true to the characters as possible, and I feel the white horse and the silver armor are more accurate representations of the real thing.  However, the ONLY way to get the Rakshas statue is to buy the limited edition!  So here comes the drama.

So, I bought the normal edition first, and eventually found and paid a TON of money for the exclusive version on Yahoo Auctions Japan.  Low and behold, when I received it, the Rakshas that was included was the Brown one, which I didn't want as much as the Black one.  At that time, I forgot there were two versions and I didn't bother asking which version was included when I bid on the auction.  Well, eventually, I was able to get the limited edition that included the Black Rakshas, but man, I was out soooo much money for some time as it was difficult to find buyers that were willing to spend the money for the statues/extras that I had to sell.  It was difficult not because I was asking for too much, but simply because not everyone in the world is open to spending so much money on Berserk collectibles like some of us are =P.

Oh well, in the end, I was able to get the versions I desired, and now I can post pictures of them in all their glory. =)
