Wyald
 - scale 1/10
- serial # 123
- limitation < 300
- estimated value: $250+
- pictures uploaded 1.22.05

Well, Wyald certainly isn't a handsome fellow, but he's one good looking STATUE, that's for sure!  It's hard to tell from the pictures, but this statue is rather large (as it should be in order to stay as true to the character as possible).  I particularly like the dog hat that he has on, his claws, and the muddy base.

This statue isn't listed on the Art of War website because the only way to get him is to be one of the first customers to preorder the Guts: Maken (Limited Edition) statue and to also send in the included-preorder ticket for Wyald.  As to why AOW decided to do this, I'm not quite sure.  As to how many Wyald statues were made, Art of War won't give me an exact number, but it's obviously less than 300 (since there are only 300 Guts: Maken (LE) statues).

However, at Comic Con 2005 in San Diego, it was announced that 30-40 Wyald statues are now available for US release so keep your eyes peeled if you want to get a good deal on one of these bad boys!  They also come with a bloody log!
