Guts: Millennium Falcon: Attack Form (Limited Edition - Bloody with Bomb)
 - scale 1/10
 - serial # 264
- limitation 300
- estimated value: $350+
- pictures uploaded 1.18.05

This statue... is truly a gorgeous piece.  I'm actually a bit surprised AOW decided to make such a dynamic sculpt, since the majority of their statues are upright, standing poses.  Nothing against the latter, but it's refreshing to see a Guts statue that shows him swinging his gigantic Dragon Slayer.  I think this statue helps demonstrate how heavy this sword really is, since his weight is completely shifted towards one side.

The extra bomb piece is pretty cool, but not the reason why I forked up the extra money to get the limited edition.  If you haven't noticed by now, I like blood, and of ALL the statues that deserve blood, it should be this one, since it's an in-action pose of Guts tearing $hit up!  Also, unlike the other Guts statues, the blood here is splashed blood, so it doesn't physically have any volume.  It's basically just flat paint.

There is also a mud base (not pictured) that helps support the end of the sword.  I didn't include it in the pictures below because I think it looks nicer without it.  Some of course, may disagree.

My advice to all of you who haven't bought this statue yet: GET IT NOW BEFORE IT SHOOTS WAY UP IN PRICE.  You know, 2-3 years from now, this piece is going to be one valuable statue!
