These little statues are actually a lot smaller than what I imagined them to be.  Each figure is about the size of the original open and closed face behelit pendants (a few inches).  But to make up for size, this set strives hard to be extra adorable... and it works!  The Puck statue with the Santa hat gives me quite a good laugh, especially when displayed next to all the other Berserk statues.  It looks... a tad out of place =P.

The wings are clear, so that's pretty neat.  However, I have to say the paint job is a TINY bit sloppy.  Maybe it's because they are so small, but I feel like Art of War could have painted these better.

But, if you're like me, and think Puck is such an endearing character (I mean, every legendary warrior has a fairy: Link in Ocarina of Time, Samanosuke in Onimusha, etc.), then the more Puck's, the better!  Agree?
