Berserk Statues (last update: 9.5.06)
  by Art of War

It took me many years to gather all the statues you see here.  Fortunately, as of Fall 2004, Art of War sells their products directly to overseas customers through their website: www.art-of-war.no-jp.  However, to acquire many of the older statues, it required a lot of painstaking effort to find them and save up enough money to even afford them, especially the exclusive limited edition models.  These editions are usually limited to only 100-300 pieces in the world!  And in reality, even the normal editions are quite scarce, usually limited to around 1000-2000 pieces in total.  In terms of value, however, often times these limited editions skyrocket in price and cost 2-3 times more than the normal ones.  What's the difference between the two versions?  Well, sometimes it's just blood on the sword and base, or a different color outfit, or a helmet, or an extra companion statue... stuff like that.  It may seem like an awfully high price to pay for these additions, but in my humble opinion, many of the limited edition models are very much worth it!
